Why Peer Programming?

The advantages of peer programming simulates a real-life work environment through interactions with a team. Pairing is when two programmers build code in tandem, through specific roles, such as driver (the writer) and navigator (the director).

Advantages

  • Encourages exchange of ideas
  • Promotes seamless workflow appointing two people two different jobs
  • Two pairs of eyes are also able to detect errors better

Driver

  • takes direction from navigator
  • writes the code
  • steers navigator back on track

Navigator

  • provides driver sense of direction based on preliminary designs
  • steers driver back on track
  • responsible for general vision of design execution
